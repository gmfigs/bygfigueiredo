London Sound Academy

London’s leading electronic music academy

London’s leading electronic music academy

Services

Web App

Web Design

Creative Strategy

Client

London Sound Academy

Location

London, England

Year

2025

Info

London Sound Academy, a leading electronic music academy in London, needed a stronger website system to support its DJ and music production courses. The project focused on building a bold, music-led digital presence with clearer course pathways, stronger tutor and studio positioning, and a booking-focused structure designed to turn aspiring artists into students.

Product thinking

The biggest challenge wasn’t adding more content... it was creating clarity. Prospective students arrived with different goals, experience levels, and expectations, making it essential to simplify the decision-making process without losing the academy’s energetic identity. Every page was designed to reduce friction, reinforce credibility through tutors and facilities, and guide users naturally toward booking a course or getting in touch.

Target Users

  • Aspiring DJs and music producers exploring professional training.

  • Beginners looking for an accessible entry point into electronic music.

  • Intermediate and advanced artists seeking to develop technical skills.

  • International students researching London’s leading music academies.

Personal Reflection

This project reinforced the importance of balancing brand expression with usability. While the visual identity needed to capture the energy of electronic music culture, the experience itself had to remain intuitive and conversion-focused. It was a valuable exercise in designing for both emotion and clarity, ensuring the brand’s personality never came at the expense of user confidence.
